Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 7, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Channel Mini Adventures ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information when you use our internal Publisher Application. Since this is a private internal tool designed exclusively for scheduling and publishing our own content, this policy is primarily provided to comply with third-party API requirements (such as TikTok, Pinterest, and YouTube).

2. Data Collection and Usage

Our internal Publisher App does not collect personal data from public users. It only uses authentication tokens belonging exclusively to the Channel Mini Adventures owner to post video files generated by our internal studio. We do not store, process, or sell any third-party user data.

3. Third-Party Services (APIs)

We use official APIs (like the TikTok API) to upload content. The application requests specific scopes (e.g., video upload and publish permissions) required to function as an automated publisher for our own profile. We do not use these APIs to retrieve or store the personal information of your users.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at our official channel email.
